About

The builder behind the brand.

A real estate practice handed down a generation, carried forward with discipline — and a conviction that good buildings are an act of service to the people who live in them.

Portrait — at work

I grew up inside a building site. Long before it was my profession, real estate was the dinner-table conversation in our home — the texture of a stone, the honesty of a layout, the trust between a developer and the families who hand over their homes for redevelopment.

When I took the practice forward as SNB Group, I wanted to keep what my family did well — relationships, patience, an eye for craft — and add to it the rigour a modern Mumbai demands: transparent processes, careful planning and precise execution on redevelopment journeys that are never simple.

Over twenty-five years we have delivered luxury residences, bungalows, villas, commercial and mixed-use spaces — from the lanes of Vile Parle to a township in Karjat. The scale changes. The standard does not.

“A home is the largest decision most families ever make. The least we owe them is the truth, and a building that ages with grace.”

Sagar Barot
